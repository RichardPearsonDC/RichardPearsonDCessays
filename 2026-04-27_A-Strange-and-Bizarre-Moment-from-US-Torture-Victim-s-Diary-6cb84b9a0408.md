### A Strange and Bizarre Moment from US Torture Victim’s Diary

Pearson sought refuge in a park but was interrupted by a strange man causing Pearson to leave. He found a sign with 666, All Seeing Eye, and “at the Mark (of the Beast)”. NPS employees said that the sign had been there for a long time. Google Maps contradicts that claim. So, what’s going on?

_This is a true story. Richard Pearson is a real person. He was tortured. He has been writing about it extensively on Medium. This essay examines a strange event one evening in Washington, DC._

### The Eye of Providence and 666 sign on National Park Service Grounds

In September 2025, Pearson was taking a rest under a tree at tennis courts in Hains Point — a peninsula that also contains a golf course and a running and bike trail. He was escaping a campaign of terror — noise harassment, street theater, and cognitive manipulation. He wrote, read, and prayed for about two hours. No one else was around his immediate vicinity. It was a true respite. The passengers and cars were 25 to 50 yards away, but it was almost as if they weren’t there at all. He enjoyed the moment.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/1*7KnJdcL87082PFj7E6RHPw.png)

Then suddenly a Black man appears. Pearson noticed him — a homeless drifter by appearance. Pearson kept a cautious eye on him. Then, the man got closer. He did some sort of dance then vanished behind a wall. It spooked Pearson, “Where did this man come from?! And why did he do that dance?” He described the dance as some sort of like Egyptian movement like one would see in ancient artifacts and tombs. “My first thought was that I need to get the hell out. My second thought was that the man needed help. I kid you not. I thought that he had escaped from somewhere where others were being held. I know. Wild thought, but he was so out-of-place, disheveled, and non-communicative that I searched for answers.”

Pearson gathered his belongings and moved away from the man. He became curious about the National Park Service buildings nearby and ventured to see what they were about. From the outside, they appeared unusual — long rows, no windows. Eventually, he ended up at the entrance.

Not much going on. The reason for no windows is because the other side is open — used for storage. Pearson kept walking.

He saw two signs in the middle of the ground — one on top of the other. “I don’t remember exactly which sign was on top, but I examined both.” Screenshots from Pearson’s videos show writings on worn wood boards.

The first sign had something like the Eye of Providence with text, “All Seeing Eye” in yellow paint, L.E.D. and an Apple with a bite taken out. The sign continued, with “WWW”, “666”, “Member of Man”, “The Beast”. On the upper right was A.T.M. — At The Mark spelled out vertically. A rough translation of the sign if that its writers believe that we have reached the biblical prophecy of the end times. A.T.M. translates to at the Mark (of the Beast).

The other sign had “No one left behind fellowship”, “Open Mic” and “on the Patomic”. Potomac, as in the Potomac River, was misspelled. This sign could also be read with a biblical translation — as in the rapture and the second coming of Christ.

The grounds were pristine. It confused Pearson how these signs could have been allowed to be in the open like that with that language.

The facilities were closing. Three Black male employees were headed to their cars nearby. Pearson asked, “How long have these signs been here?!” One replied, “Oh those? They have been there for a long time.” Pearson took this to mean years. By the look of the aging wood, at least 8 years.

This conclusion persisted until late April 2026. Google Maps does not establish that they have been there a long time.

They are there in August 2025 — a month before Pearson discovers them.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/1*rcqBd0-nshGmLOUUBLD9ZQ.png)

They may or may not appear to be there in November 2024 — leaves covered the ground. However, leaves also appear on the ground in August 2025. The signs were clearly visible then.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/1*PPFRytdiwtzYbtaWozrD7A.png)

But they definitely not there in July 2023.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/1*YDmTQ6mYN9yaPlRxBuRUYA.png)

So what is a “long time”? According to Google Maps, less than a year.

#### So what does Pearson make of this?

“First, I think that those signs were placed there in 2025. There is no way that they could not have been noticed and trashed given the pristine grounds.

Second, at that point, I knew that pinning or “behavioral steering” was possible. You should look up the MIND Act that was proposed in the US Senate in September 2025. It’s basically admitting that such technology exists. It involves overriding conscious decision-making to essentially place someone at a location. It can do lots of things. One is interfering with the hippocampus — the mind’s GPS — to move people. It happened to me countless times. I must have stalled too much at the tree, so they decided to use the man to put me on the move. That is what explains the man. I have seen that reinforcement too — using street players to move other street players. They both may be under behavioral manipulation.

The people behind it see it as a game. They do. Grave violations with cognitive liberty and constitutional rights — and they see it as a game. Real psychos.

Now, why the sign? That is a good question. Even if we are at the end times and the Anti-Christ is among us, what the hell does it have to do with me? Leave me alone. The other thought was that this is just the type of sick humor that psychos enjoy. I will be glad to see these people exposed, convicted, jailed, and medicated — and for someone to finally take these remote controls out of their hands.”

‘A.T.M. — At The Mark.’ At the Mark of the Beast.
