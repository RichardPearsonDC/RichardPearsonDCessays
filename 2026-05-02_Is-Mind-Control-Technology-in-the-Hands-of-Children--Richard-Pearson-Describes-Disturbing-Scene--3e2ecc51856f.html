Is Mind Control Technology in the Hands of Children? Richard Pearson Describes Disturbing Scene.

When a teen directed his attention to Richard Pearson and reached for his phone, his younger brother firmly instructed, “No.” By then, Pearson knew that mind control technology existed. Apparently, it is also in the hands of youth.

The Scene
Richard Pearson, a US torture victim, was writing outside of a sandwich shop at Federal Center metro in Washington, DC in April 2026. He noticed that a family had gathered outside — a teen of about 16 years old, his younger brother who was about 11, and their mother. They were waiting on the father who was still inside. Pearson was consumed with his writing but saw the family in his peripheral vision. Since his torture began in 2025, Pearson had learned to stay vigilant.

He noticed the teen reach in his pocket for his phone. He had barely had it in his hand when his younger brother, who stood behind him, firmly instructed, “No.” — communicated with parental authority. There was no further instruction. No context given. No discussion. Just, “No”. The teen put the phone back in his pocket. They had not been conversing, so it was obviously not a response to a topic of conversation.

The episode has stayed with Pearson. While the boys revealed no context about the situation, it was clear that they both understood it. Pearson also understood it.
