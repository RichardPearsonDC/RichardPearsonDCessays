Man Taunts US Torture Victim with “We’re in Control” T-shirt After He Returns to US

Pearson returned to the US after fleeing from Canada and came face-to-face with a white male with “We’re in Control” T-shirt at Union Station

Dr. Richard Pearson, US torture victim, returned from Canada after he attempted asylum. Elsewhere on Medium, Pearson wrote about his experience in Canada that caused him to flee Canada in less than 48 hours — leaving behind his US passport to cross the US-Canadian border on foot in desperation.

Pearson left the Union Station bus terminal on March 29th around mid-afternoon. He took a break just outside of Union Station, standing next to the column. The AI-generated image below is his position (not the actual person). Within minutes, a white male (mid to late 20s) walked in Pearson’s direct path within 6–7 feet and made eye contact. His plain white T-shirt with black font read, “We’re in control”. No design. Just plain text on a plain shirt.

To understand the significance of this man’s T-shirt, one has to understand Pearson’s experience with torture, street theater, and harassment starting in 2025 in Washington, DC. “These plain T-shirts is a main mode of communication. They use quick printing to terrorize people. Typically, they are black with white font. Sometimes, it’s the reverse, as in this case. They look innocent on the surface, but the whole point is to convey a message to a ‘target’,” said Pearson. “I have documented this extensively.”

Pearson regretted not taking a photo in the moment but has a good reason. “If I was constantly taking pictures, I would be in a state of high stress. My cortisol levels would not be sustainable from a health perspective. I decided several months ago that I needed to take less pictures.” Still, surveillance video would show the man with the T-shirt.

What Did the Message Mean?

By the time that Pearson saw the man, he had been in what he considers “Nazi America” for a full year. He had been pinned — a term that he uses to describe being pulled to a location without conscious awareness or control. “It’s just like the video game Sims — using remote control to move people and make them do stuff. They had me sit causing a chemical assault. We shouldn’t be surprised at all about this stuff really. You have had scores of victims and whistleblowers, including high-level officials, talking about government use of this kind of “mind control” technology on US citizens on US soil for at least 30 years. We have just failed to take them seriously. Congress has failed to act. In 2025, you had a leading and highly respected neuroscientist, who had worked with the government on this exact technology, to say on his LinkedIn that the technology exists — ‘loosed into the world’, as he says.” The scientist used “mind control” and “mind reading”.

Pearson had a one-time cognitive intrusion when a voice entered his skull using microwave technology — the same technology that is used in museums. He does not feel any shame in telling people that he heard a voice. “A simple Google search will tell you that putting a voice in someone’s head is relatively simple. Two ultrasonic waves below human hearing come together in the skull. The difference of the wavelength is the voice. The person targeted with the beam is the only one who hears it. Companies make these products. Museums use the technology. Americans who are too distracted to understand where science is — and how their government is abusing technology.”

Pearson was physically assaulted. He was falsely arrested — the police manufactured a third witness, then instructed him not to show his arrest record to the court. Trump federalized the DC police force, which allowed them to operate under federal auspices and all that comes with it — technology, resources, incentives, and federal legal doctrines of protection and immunity. He was stalked, harassed, and sleep deprived. His apartment was broken into. His privacy invaded. His computer hacked. And he wrote about — scattered his writings, photos, and videos all over the Internet.

So, when the man appeared with the T-shirt, “We’re in control,” Pearson was not surprised. “You have to understand the psychosis here because there is a real mental condition— disassociation from and dichotomization of reality, disregard for the law and US Constitution, diminished executive control in the brain, and exclusive focus on power and control of other people and society. I don’t say this to vilify or de-humanize them. I am saying that we are dealing with a mental and neurological crisis within and outside of government that causes them to stalk and terrorize. They feel not just empowered, but without rules, accountability, or moral constraint.”

Pearson believes that the man could have been referring to any number of mechanisms of control. The most serious is mind control. “I am not talking about conspiracy. I am also not talking about anything that isn’t well-documented. I am a researcher. I am talking about what I know to be true. It is a fact that I have been pinned on numerous occasions — feeling a bodily pull to go somewhere where I have no desire to go, only to discover evidence of their calling card. The voice-to-skull transmission is altogether another form of mind control.”

Pearson believes that he has seen disturbing evidence in Washington, DC of Black men being stalked. The video shows these men — two different men in the same video frame being followed by people in red shirts — a key calling card in the campaign that targeted him. These colors appeared frequently, but, most disturbingly, with a cluster at Eastern Market. “There is no ordinary explanation why these two Black men would be stalked. There is no ordinary explanation why their stalkers would both have red shirts of the same shade. We must consider alternatives and an alternative must include something disturbing and surreal — but that people have been ringing the alarm about for decades.”
